## Description
Durra is a project developed to meet the requirements of the IT College senior project course, and is guided by the vision of preserving Bahrain's jewelry heritage and culture. The word "Durra" is the arabic pronunciation of pearl and things of value, and was chosen to intitle the web-based platform matching between jewelrs and jewelry seekers in Bahrain. -update to include features-
